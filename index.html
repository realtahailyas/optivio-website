The Optivio Scale System

The exact 3-phase framework we've used across every account we manage. No theory — just the structure behind $540K+ in tracked revenue.

1
WEEK 1–2

Account Rebuild & Audience Architecture

We audit your pixel data, restructure your campaigns into proper TOF/MOF/BOF layers, and map your buyer journey before a single dollar is spent on new creative. Most accounts are bleeding budget here without knowing it.

2
WEEK 3–6

Rapid Creative Testing & CPA Discovery

We test 4–8 creatives across hooks, formats, and angles — then kill losers within 72 hours. The goal: find your sub-$15 CPA winner. In our last account, this phase alone dropped CPA from $27 to $13.28.

3
WEEK 7–12

Controlled Scale & Second Funnel Build

Winners get fuel. We scale spend 20–30% weekly while monitoring CPA drift. When the first funnel saturates, we build a second — new LAL seeds, new TOF, parallel structure. This is how we scaled one account from 1.6x to 4.18x ROAS in 90 days.

Common Questions

How much do you charge?

We offer two models: a flat monthly retainer, or a hybrid model (lower base + performance bonus tied to CPA or ROAS targets). There's a one-time setup fee to cover account audit, pixel/CAPI verification, and campaign architecture. Exact pricing depends on your region and account complexity — we'll walk through options on the audit call.

What if I already have an agency running my ads?

That's fine. Many of our clients came from other agencies. The audit call will show you exactly what your current setup is missing — even if you decide to stay with them.

How fast will I see results?

Most brands see meaningful CPA improvement within 3–4 weeks of restructuring. Full-scale results typically show by month 2–3. Anyone promising results in 7 days is lying to you.

Do you handle creative production?

We don't produce creative in-house, but we direct it. You'll get a detailed creative brief every test cycle — exact hooks, formats, angles, and references. You produce or we help you find someone who can.

What if it doesn't work?

We're month-to-month. If performance isn't tracking after 60 days, we'll have an honest conversation about whether to continue. No guilt trips, no hard sell to extend. If it's not working, we'll tell you first.

Do you work with brands outside Dubai?

Yes. We manage accounts for eCommerce brands in the UAE, Australia, Canada, and the US. Meta Ads Manager doesn't care about time zones — and neither do we.
